Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is a Korean rice dish. It is served as a bowl of warm white rice topped with namul and gochujang. Egg and sliced meat are common additions, stirred together thoroughly just before eating. The term bibim means "mixing" and bap is cooked rice.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Eggs
  • Gochujang
  • Cucumbers

Steps

  1. Prep Ingredients
  2. Mix together
